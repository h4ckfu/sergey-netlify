Global warming, natural gas, and the internet have created giant fissures capable of swallowing mining towns whole.

When that mining cavity begins to erode, a kind of vertical vent is created through the earth; turbulent air explosively released through the eroded hole, as if it were a cosmic geyser spewing trees of debris incessantly over the employee parking lot strewn with bodies.

Lucky passersby escaping with light injuries while others have to be freed by metal jaws, later to have legs amputated. Still others swallowed whole, intact and unharmed, returning back to the soil.
